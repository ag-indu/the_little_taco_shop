Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United States?

Tacos first appeared in the United States around 1905. This is when Mexican migrants began immigrating to the U.S. and brought their food traditions with them, including tacos. Initially, tacos were considered a lower-class food, associated with Mexican migrants working in mines and on railroads.
